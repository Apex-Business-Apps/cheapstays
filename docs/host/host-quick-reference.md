# Host Quick Reference

Key rules at a glance. Full detail in the [Host Handbook](./host-handbook.md).

---

## Before You List

| Requirement | What you need |
|-------------|--------------|
| Legal authority | Ownership, subletting permission in writing, or signed agent authorization |
| Accurate address | Full street address including unit number |
| Local permit | Include registration/license number in listing if required by local law |
| Occupancy limit | State maximum overnight guests; must comply with local fire code |
| Insurance | Recommended — standard home policies often exclude paying guests |

---

## What Must Be in Your Listing

- Quiet hours and noise rules
- Building / HOA rules that affect guests
- Pet and smoking policy
- Party / event prohibition (if applicable)
- All exterior cameras and smart doorbell cameras
- Smart home devices with microphones or cameras
- Third-party access during stays (cleaner, manager, building staff)
- Pool, hot tub, balcony, or other high-risk features

---

## Recording Rules — Hard Lines

| Location | Rule |
|----------|------|
| Bedrooms | No cameras or microphones. Ever. |
| Bathrooms | No cameras or microphones. Ever. |
| Interior common areas | No cameras or microphones. Ever. |
| Exterior / front door | Permitted — must be disclosed in listing before booking |
| Concealed anywhere | Prohibited. No exceptions. |

---

## Entry During a Booking

You may enter only if:

1. The guest has given explicit consent, **or**
2. There is a genuine emergency (fire, flood, gas leak) — notify the guest as soon as safely possible.

Unauthorized entry may result in a full guest refund and account action.

---

## If an Incident Happens

1. **Emergency?** Call emergency services first. Not CheapStays.
2. **Crime?** Call police. Get a report number.
3. **Document** — photograph before cleanup; write down what happened and when.
4. **Report to CheapStays** within 48 hours via the incident reporting tool.
5. **Retain evidence** (photos, messages, receipts) for at least 12 months.

CheapStays preserves platform records and cooperates with authorities where legally required. It does not investigate crimes, make fault determinations, or act as an emergency responder.

---

## What CheapStays Is Not

| Not a... | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Insurer | It does not cover your property or liability |
| Landlord | It has no authority over your lease or building |
| Emergency responder | Call 911 (or local equivalent) — not the Help Center |
| Property manager | Day-to-day operations are your responsibility |
| Law enforcement | Report crimes to police, not to CheapStays |

---

## Minimum Safety Equipment

| Item | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Smoke alarm | One per level, tested and functional |
| CO detector | Required with gas, oil heat, or attached garage |
| Fire extinguisher | One accessible unit near the kitchen |
| Bedroom exits | Opens from inside without a key or code |
| Emergency numbers | Posted inside the unit |
| Host emergency line | 24/7 number provided to guests |
