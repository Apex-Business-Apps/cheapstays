# Host Safety Checklist

Use this checklist before your first booking and after any gap in hosting. Keep a dated copy for your records.

---

## Fire and Life Safety

- [ ] Smoke alarm on every level — tested in the last 30 days
- [ ] Carbon monoxide detector present (required if gas appliances, oil heat, or attached garage)
- [ ] Fire extinguisher accessible near the kitchen — not expired
- [ ] Every bedroom has an exit that opens from the inside without a key or code
- [ ] Hallways and exit routes are clear of furniture, boxes, and stored items
- [ ] Local emergency numbers (fire, police, ambulance) posted inside the unit
- [ ] 24/7 emergency contact number for the property provided to guests

## Building / Condo / HOA (skip if not applicable)

- [ ] Building emergency procedures documented and available to guests at check-in
- [ ] Building emergency contact number included in guest information
- [ ] Evacuation routes and assembly points identified for your floor or unit
- [ ] HOA or building rules that affect guests disclosed in the listing

## Legal and Compliance

- [ ] Legal authority to list confirmed (ownership, subletting permission, or agent authorization)
- [ ] Full, accurate street address (including unit number) on the listing
- [ ] Short-term rental permit or license number included in the listing where required by local law
- [ ] Occupancy limit stated in the listing and compliant with local fire code

## Disclosure — Listing Accuracy

- [ ] Quiet hours and noise restrictions stated in the listing
- [ ] Common area rules (pool, gym, parking) disclosed
- [ ] Pet and smoking policies stated
- [ ] Any prohibition on parties or events stated
- [ ] Third-party access during stays (cleaner, manager, building staff) disclosed

## Recording and Surveillance

- [ ] No cameras, microphones, or sensors in any bedroom, bathroom, or private interior space
- [ ] No concealed recording devices anywhere on the property
- [ ] Exterior cameras (if any) disclosed in the listing before booking
- [ ] Smart doorbells with video disclosed; not directed into interior spaces
- [ ] Smart home devices with microphones or cameras disclosed in the listing

## Physical Safety Features

- [ ] Known hazards disclosed in the listing
- [ ] Pool, hot tub, or balcony safety features in place and compliant with local law
- [ ] Weapons (if any) secured in a locked safe; ammunition stored separately; disclosed in listing
- [ ] Property free of active pest infestation

## Digital Safety

- [ ] Guest Wi-Fi network separate from any network connected to security cameras or smart devices
- [ ] No monitoring or interception of guest device traffic
