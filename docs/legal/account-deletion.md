# Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time. If you have upcoming confirmed bookings, cancel or honour them before requesting deletion.

## How to Request Deletion

1. Email [cheapstays.me@gmail.com](mailto:cheapstays.me@gmail.com) from your registered account email address.
2. Include your display name and confirm that you want your account permanently deleted.
3. We will verify your identity and process the request within **30 days**.
4. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

## What Gets Deleted

| Data | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Account profile (name, avatar, bio) | Deleted |
| Active listings | Removed from public search |
| Saved preferences and favourites | Deleted |
| Active sessions | Revoked immediately |
| AI concierge conversation logs | Deleted after 90-day retention window |

## What We Must Retain

Some records cannot be deleted because we are legally required to keep them:

| Data | Retention period | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Booking records | 5 years | Tax and legal obligation |
| Payment references | 5 years | Financial records |
| Host verification photos | Verification outcome + 1 year | Identity audit trail |
| Support tickets | 2 years | Dispute resolution |

Retained records are kept securely and are not used for any commercial purpose. Anonymised aggregate data with no individual identifiers may be retained indefinitely for product analytics.

## Re-Registration

Deleting your account removes your access permanently. If you wish to use CheapStays again after deletion, you will need to create a new account. Accounts deleted for policy violations may not be re-registered.
