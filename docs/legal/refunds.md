# Refund Policy

All prices on CheapStays are in Philippine Pesos (₱). Payments are processed by PayMongo — a BSP-regulated Philippine processor — and support GCash, Maya, debit/credit cards, and bank transfer. Refund eligibility depends on the cancellation policy attached to each listing.

## 1. Cancellation Tiers

Each listing displays one of three standard cancellation tiers, or a custom policy set by the host. Read the policy on the listing page **before** you book.

| Tier | Refund eligibility |
|---|---|
| **Flexible** | Full refund if cancelled ≥ 24 hours before check-in |
| **Moderate** | Full refund if cancelled ≥ 5 days before check-in |
| **Strict** | 50% refund if cancelled ≥ 7 days before check-in; no refund within 7 days |

Host-set custom cancellation policies are displayed at checkout and override the standard tiers above. The platform fee is **non-refundable** in all cases, except where a platform error caused the booking to fail.

## 2. How to Cancel

- Cancel through the CheapStays platform, not exclusively by contacting the host.
- Cancel before the policy deadline to receive any refund you are entitled to.

## 3. Refund Timelines

Refunds are returned to the original payment method:

- **GCash / Maya** — typically same-day or instant.
- **Credit / Debit Card** — 3–7 business days.
- **Bank Transfer** — subject to your bank's processing time.

## 4. No-Show Policy

If you do not arrive and do not cancel, you forfeit the full booking amount. A no-show is recorded in the system and is not eligible for a refund.

CheapStays will not override the no-show policy except in documented force-majeure situations (declared natural disaster, government travel ban, or medical emergency with contemporaneous evidence). Contact support within **48 hours** of the missed check-in with documentation.

## 5. Material Discrepancy Claims

If the property is materially different from its listing, report it through the CheapStays support system within **24 hours of check-in**. Document issues with photos or video at the time of discovery. CheapStays will investigate and may offer a partial refund, rebooking assistance, or other remedy at its discretion.

## 6. Damage Claims

Normal use is expected. Damage beyond ordinary wear and tear is the guest's financial responsibility.

**Guests** — report any damage to the host and to CheapStays support within **24 hours of check-out**.

**Hosts** — file a damage claim within **48 hours of check-out** with:
- Timestamped photographic evidence
- Itemised repair or replacement costs
- Receipts where available

Claims filed more than 48 hours after check-out may not be actioned. CheapStays mediates damage claims but does not guarantee recovery for either party.

## 7. Chargebacks

Before filing a chargeback with your bank or card issuer, submit a dispute through [cheapstays.me@gmail.com](mailto:cheapstays.me@gmail.com) and allow us **72 hours** to respond. Filing a fraudulent chargeback is a breach of these terms and may result in account suspension.
