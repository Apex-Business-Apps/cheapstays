# Renter Rules

These rules apply to all guests booking through CheapStays. A confirmed booking is a binding agreement between you and the host.

## 1. Making a Booking

When you request a booking the platform creates a payment intent. The booking becomes confirmed once the host accepts — or immediately for instant-book listings.

- Provide accurate check-in and check-out dates.
- Declare the correct number of guests; do not exceed the listing's maximum.
- Read the full listing description, photos, and house rules before booking.
- Submitting a booking request is an offer; confirmation by the host (or instant-book) creates a binding contract.

CheapStays displays listing content as provided by hosts. We do not independently verify every photo, amenity, or description. If you arrive and find a material discrepancy, report it through the support system **within 24 hours of check-in**.

## 2. Guest Conduct at the Property

You are a guest, not a tenant. You have permission to occupy the space for the booked period only, under the conditions the host has set.

- Follow the house rules published in the listing.
- Keep noise levels reasonable; respect neighbours.
- Do not exceed the declared guest count.
- Do not smoke in non-smoking properties.
- Do not hold parties, events, or commercial activities without the host's explicit written consent.
- Do not use the property for any unlawful purpose.
- Leave the property in the same condition you found it. Reasonable wear is expected; damage is not.

## 3. Check-In and Check-Out

Check-in and check-out times are set by the host.

- Honour the check-in and check-out times stated in your booking confirmation.
- Request early check-in or late check-out from the host **before** your stay — not at the door.
- Vacate the property by the stated check-out time. Overstaying without the host's written consent may result in additional charges and account review.

## 4. Cancellations and Refunds

Each listing displays a cancellation policy set by the host. Read it before you book — CheapStays enforces it.

- Cancel through the CheapStays platform, not exclusively by contacting the host.
- Cancel before the policy's deadline to receive any refund you are entitled to.
- No-shows (failing to arrive without cancelling) are not eligible for a refund.

The platform fee is non-refundable unless CheapStays caused the booking to fail. For the full cancellation tier table and refund timelines, see the [Refund Policy](/refunds).

## 5. Damage and Incidentals

If you damage something beyond normal wear and tear, you are responsible for the cost.

- Report any damage or breakage to the host and to CheapStays support within **24 hours of check-out**.
- Do not conceal damage.
- Cooperate with any damage-assessment process.

Hosts must provide documented evidence (photos, receipts) to file a claim. Normal wear and tear does not constitute a damage claim.

## 6. Disputes and Emergencies

If something goes wrong — the property is not as described, the host is unresponsive, or there is a safety concern — tell us immediately.

- Document issues with photos or video at the time of discovery.
- Report a material discrepancy through the CheapStays support system within **24 hours of check-in**.
- For emergencies (fire, medical, crime) call Philippine emergency services first: **911**.

CheapStays will investigate material-discrepancy claims and may offer a partial refund, rebooking assistance, or other remedy at its discretion. Our target response time for urgent issues is within **24 hours**.

## 7. Reviews

After your stay you may leave an honest review. Reviews are public and help the whole community.

- Post only honest, first-hand reviews of your actual stay.
- Do not post reviews in exchange for payment, discounts, or favours.
- Do not post defamatory, harassing, or knowingly false statements.

CheapStays may remove reviews that violate community standards. We do not remove negative reviews simply because a host requests it. One review is permitted per completed booking.
