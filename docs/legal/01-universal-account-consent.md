## Plain-Language Summary

This is the foundation agreement every person must accept before using CheapStays — as a guest, host, or member. It covers who is allowed to create an account, how your identity is protected, what conduct is expected, and which law governs disputes. Every other CheapStays document builds on this one. If anything here conflicts with a topic-specific document, the topic-specific document controls for that topic.

---

## 1. Who May Use CheapStays

### What this means
CheapStays connects guests who want short-term stays with hosts who list properties. To use it you must be a real adult acting for yourself, or a registered business acting through an authorised representative.

### Your obligation
- You are at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher).
- You are a real person or registered legal entity — no bots, synthetic identities, or proxy accounts.
- You have not been previously removed from CheapStays for a policy violation.
- You provide accurate personal information and keep it current.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays does not independently verify the age or identity of every user at sign-up. We rely on your honest declaration and reserve the right to request verification documents at any time. We are not liable for harm caused by a user who provided false information.

---

## 2. One Account, One Identity

### What this means
Each person or entity gets one account. Running multiple accounts to game search rankings, reviews, or platform policies is prohibited.

### Your obligation
- Maintain only one active account per identity.
- Do not share login credentials with others.
- Notify us immediately at **cheapstays.me@gmail.com** if you believe your account has been accessed without your permission.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays cannot monitor all login activity in real time. We rely on you to report suspicious access. We are not responsible for losses caused by credential theft where you failed to notify us promptly.

---

## 3. Account Security

### What this means
You are responsible for keeping your account secure. CheapStays uses magic-link email authentication (no passwords), but your email account must be guarded too.

### Your obligation
- Keep your registered email account secure with a strong password and, ideally, two-factor authentication on your email provider.
- Log out of shared or public devices.
- Do not click sign-in links from emails you did not initiate.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays sends sign-in links only in response to an explicit request on the sign-in page. We will never ask for your sign-in link, a one-time code, or any credential via phone call, SMS, or social media. Any such request is a scam — do not comply.

---

## 4. Community Standards

### What this means
CheapStays is a community. Conduct that harms other users, hosts, guests, or the integrity of the platform will result in suspension or permanent removal.

### Your obligation
You agree **not** to:
- Harass, threaten, or discriminate against any person based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, nationality, or any other protected characteristic.
- Post false reviews, manipulate ratings, or engage in any fraudulent activity.
- Attempt to move transactions off the platform to avoid fees.
- Use CheapStays for any unlawful purpose.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays moderates reported conduct and acts on clear violations. We are not a law-enforcement body and cannot guarantee that every policy violation is detected or actioned in real time.

---

## 5. Changes to These Documents

### What this means
We will update these legal documents when law, product, or policy changes. We will give you notice. If you keep using CheapStays after the effective date, you accept the updated version.

### Your obligation
- Keep your email address current so notices reach you.
- Review the updated documents when notified.
- Stop using CheapStays before the new effective date if you do not accept the changes.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays will give at least **14 days' advance notice** before material changes take effect, via email and in-app notice. Changes will not be applied retroactively to completed bookings.

---

## 6. Termination

### What this means
Either party may end the relationship, subject to any in-progress bookings.

### Your obligation
- You may delete your account at any time via account settings or by emailing **cheapstays.me@gmail.com**.
- If you have upcoming confirmed bookings, cancel or honour them before requesting deletion.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays may suspend or terminate your account immediately for serious violations (fraud, harassment, illegal activity). For lesser violations we will attempt to notify you and provide an opportunity to respond before taking action. We retain the right to keep anonymised records for legal and safety purposes after your account is deleted.

---

## 7. Governing Law & Disputes

### What this means
These documents are governed by Alberta law (Canada) for entity-level matters, and by applicable Philippine law for in-country property transactions. Disputes are resolved through the platform's support process first, then mediation, then the courts.

### Your obligation
- Raise disputes through the CheapStays support system before pursuing external remedies.
- For Philippine property disputes, local barangay mediation or the DHSUD process may apply.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays is a marketplace intermediary, not a party to the accommodation contract between host and guest. We will act as a neutral facilitator in disputes and may enforce outcomes through platform tools (e.g., holding a refund pending investigation).
