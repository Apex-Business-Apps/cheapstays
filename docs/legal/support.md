# Support

Need help with a booking, a listing, your account, or a dispute? Here is how to reach us and what to expect.

## Contact

**Email:** [cheapstays.me@gmail.com](mailto:cheapstays.me@gmail.com)

Include your **booking ID** (or ticket number if you have one) and any relevant screenshots or links so we can investigate quickly. We will confirm receipt and provide a ticket number.

**Response time:** within 48 hours for standard issues. Urgent safety issues are prioritised.

For emergencies — fire, medical, crime — call Philippine emergency services first: **911**.

## What We Can Help With

| Topic | What to include |
|---|---|
| Booking issue (wrong dates, no confirmation) | Booking ID, check-in/check-out dates |
| Property not as described | Photos, booking ID, date/time of discovery |
| Refund or cancellation | Booking ID, cancellation date, payment method |
| Damage claim (hosts) | Booking ID, timestamped photos, itemised costs |
| Account access problem | Your registered email address |
| Suspected scam or fraud | Screenshots of the communication |
| Safety or surveillance concern | Property address, booking ID, photos if safe to take |
| Host application or verification | Your submitted documents and any rejection message |

## Escalation Keywords

Support tickets that contain the words *refund, fraud, chargeback, legal*, or *lawsuit* are automatically flagged and routed to a human agent — they bypass AI handling entirely.

## Dispute Process

Before filing a chargeback with your bank, submit a dispute through support and allow us **72 hours** to respond. For property disputes, file within **24 hours of check-in** (material discrepancy) or **48 hours of check-out** (damage).
