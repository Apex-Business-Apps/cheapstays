## Plain-Language Summary

This document is for people who want to list properties on CheapStays and earn rental income. To become a host you must verify your identity, hold the legal right to rent out the property, list it accurately, and honour every confirmed booking. CheapStays charges a platform fee, takes care of the payment flow, and may suspend or remove listings that violate these rules.

---

## 1. Eligibility to Host

### What this means
Not everyone can list a property. You need the legal right to rent it out and you must pass our identity-verification process.

### Your obligation
- You are the property owner, an authorised leaseholder, or you hold written permission from the owner to sublet.
- You have obtained (or are not required to obtain) any local government permits, zoning clearances, and homeowners-association approvals needed to operate a short-term rental.
- You are at least 18 years old and hold a valid government-issued photo ID.
- Your property is located in the Republic of the Philippines.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays performs identity verification (government ID + selfie match) but does not verify property title documents or local-permit status. You are solely responsible for the legal permissibility of your listing. CheapStays will cooperate with authorities if a listing is found to violate Philippine law.

---

## 2. Verification Process

### What this means
Before your listing goes live we confirm you are who you say you are. Verification is required; no active listing can go live without it.

### Your obligation
- Upload a clear, valid government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's licence, or Philippine national ID).
- Upload a matching selfie (taken at the time of submission).
- Provide an accurate phone number and city/region.
- Respond to any follow-up from our verification team within **5 business days**.

### Platform boundary
Verification status follows: `unverified → pending → verified` (or `rejected`). Only `verified` hosts may publish active listings. CheapStays targets a **3–5 business day** turnaround for standard submissions. A rejection notice will state the reason. Approved verification does not expire unless your account is flagged for re-verification.

---

## 3. Listing Accuracy

### What this means
Guests book based on what you show them. If your listing misleads guests, you bear the consequences.

### Your obligation
- Provide photos that honestly represent the current condition of the property.
- List only amenities that are actually available; remove any amenity the moment it becomes unavailable.
- Set your maximum-guest count to a number the property can safely and comfortably accommodate.
- Disclose all house rules (noise curfew, pets, smoking, events) clearly in the listing.
- Disclose any security cameras in common areas (see Safety, Privacy & Surveillance Bundle, Section 6).
- Keep your calendar up to date to avoid double-bookings.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays may audit listings for policy compliance and request edits. We may suppress listings that receive multiple substantiated guest reports of inaccuracy. AI-generated listing descriptions (via the ai-describe tool) are drafts — you remain responsible for their accuracy before publishing.

---

## 4. Honouring Bookings

### What this means
A confirmed booking is your commitment to that guest. Cancelling or failing to show up causes real harm.

### Your obligation
- Honour all confirmed bookings.
- Ensure the property is clean, safe, and accessible at the agreed check-in time.
- Provide all amenities listed as functional at the time of booking.
- Respond to guest messages within **24 hours** — this directly affects your response-rate score.
- If you must cancel a confirmed booking, notify the guest and CheapStays as early as possible, provide a full refund, and state the reason.

### Platform boundary
Host cancellations are tracked and affect search ranking. CheapStays reserves the right to levy a cancellation penalty for host-initiated cancellations within **7 days** of check-in. The current penalty schedule is displayed in host settings. Penalties are deducted from future payouts.

## 5. Guest Safety & Property Standards

### What this means
Guests trust that your property is safe. Basic safety requirements are non-negotiable.

### Your obligation
- Ensure smoke detectors and at least one fire extinguisher are present and functional.
- Provide access to clean drinking water, or clearly disclose in the listing if tap water is not potable.
- Ensure structural elements (stairs, balconies, railings) are in safe condition.
- Disclose hazards (unfenced pool, steep terrain, nearby construction) in the listing.
- Keep emergency exit paths clear at all times.

### Platform boundary
CheapStays does not inspect properties. We act on host declarations and guest reports. Properties with substantiated safety violations are suspended pending host remediation. CheapStays is not liable for guest injuries caused by undisclosed or unresolved hazards in a host's property.

---

## 6. Platform Fees & Payouts

### What this means
CheapStays charges a percentage fee on each completed booking. You receive the remainder after the fee.

### Your obligation
- Accept the fee schedule displayed in host settings at the time of listing publication. The applicable rate is the rate in effect when the booking was confirmed.
- Declare and pay applicable income tax on rental earnings — CheapStays does not withhold or remit Philippine tax on your behalf.
- Register with the BIR if required under Philippine tax law.

### Platform boundary
Payouts are released after guest check-out, subject to any dispute-hold periods. The fee schedule may change with **30 days' advance notice**. CheapStays does not provide tax or legal advice.

## 7. Suspension & Removal

### What this means
CheapStays can remove your listing or account for policy violations.

### Your obligation
- Comply with all platform policies, including updates made after your first listing.
- Cooperate with any investigation.
- Do not re-list under a different account after suspension.

### Platform boundary
Suspension may be temporary (pending investigation) or permanent. Serious violations — fraud, endangering guests, illegal activity — result in immediate suspension without prior notice. For lesser violations we will notify you and allow an opportunity to respond. Suspension decisions may be appealed by emailing **cheapstays.me@gmail.com**.
